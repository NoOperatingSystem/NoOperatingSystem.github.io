Features

Free to use

Enterprise developer, corporation or ordinary user? In NoOS it doesn't matter. We focus on reliable and free software for everyone.

Moreover, our NoLicence provides you lifelong support.

Virus resistant

NoOS is the first and only 100% virus resistant operating system. Fear no more dangerous hackers, trojans and other spyware/malware.

No firewall or antivirus programs required, everything you need is provided by NoOS kernel.

Always up-to-date

No more annoying updates and computer restarts. NoOS is always up-to-date and requires no assistance when updating.

Focus on your work, play games, do whatever you want. Don't waste time with updates anymore.

No click boot-up in no time

Start your NoOS using no button. The system itself recognize the right time to boot up.

Use your favourite device as soon as you want to. Don't wait valuable seconds anymore.

Hardware compatibility

In NoOS we see no difference in Mac, PC or mobile devices. Our system runs literally on every possible piece of hardware.

In addition, NoOS is capable of recognizing hardware component failure and simulate its behaviour. You can even remove CPU while NoOS is running! Even if CPU is present, NoOS assures it never overheats.

NoOS is absolutely quiet. We believe that quit environment is key to maximal user experience.

Get mobile!

NoOS has been tested on all possible mobile devices and we must say it's amazing. Prolong your battery life up to 20.000% only by using NoOS.

Try it out! You will love NoOS from the first moment.

Get maximum of your 4k display

Currently NoOS supports up to sixteen 4k displays. Play games, do graphics or simply watch movies in 4k.

We don't want our users struggle with low-resolution displays.

No bugs

NoOS is 100% bug-free. We are absolutely sure that all features have been properly tested on every possible hardware configuration.

NoOS is most appropriate operating system whenever you require reliability. Run it on servers, desktops, mobile devices or even your washing machine.

With NoOS you don't need to worry about your sensitive personal data such as photos, videos or company data.

Installation

Installation is really simple. Please follow these steps:
  1. Click Install button
  2. Read carefully NoLicence
  3. Agree to NoLicence
  4. NoOS will be downloaded and installed immediately
  5. Turn off your device.
  6. Now NoOS is installed and ready to use. Remember, you can always run any other operating system that is currently installed simply by pressing the power button. Anyway, we believe you will never have to do that again. Ever.
